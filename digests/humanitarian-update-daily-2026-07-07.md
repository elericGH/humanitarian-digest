## 🇦🇺 Australian Priority Alerts
No new items.

## 🌏 Tier 1 — Pacific & Asia-Pacific
Super Typhoon Bavi has intensified dramatically, with [GDACS tropical cyclone tracking reporting sustained winds of 287 km/h as of 06:00 UTC on 6 July](http://gdacs.org/report.aspx?eventid=1001279&episodeid=22&eventtype=TC). The [Pacific Disaster Center continues to issue active advisories for the storm](https://disasteralert.pdc.org/disasteralert/?hazard_id=476585), which is among the most intense systems recorded in the basin this season. Communities in the storm's projected path should monitor official warnings closely over the coming days.

A magnitude 5.8 earthquake struck the Philippines at 08:11 UTC on 6 July, [according to GDACS](http://gdacs.org/report.aspx?eventid=1550395&episodeid=1716555&eventtype=EQ). The tremor comes as the country is still responding to the [June 2026 Philippines earthquake and tsunami emergency, which ReliefWeb lists as an ongoing disaster](https://reliefweb.int/disaster/eq-2026-000083-phl), raising concerns about cumulative strain on local response capacity.

Tropical Cyclone Maysak (93 km/h as of 4 July) also remains on [GDACS's active tropical cyclone list](http://gdacs.org/report.aspx?eventid=1001281&episodeid=13&eventtype=TC) in the same period, alongside continuing recovery operations for Typhoon Sinlaku in the Federated States of Micronesia, an [ongoing disaster per ReliefWeb](https://reliefweb.int/disaster/tc-2026-000050-fsm).

## 🌍 Tier 2 — Middle East & Africa
Israeli bombing killed and injured civilians in Lebanon and Gaza on 6 July, with the UN reporting that [Israeli restrictions and border closures continue to block aid deliveries into Gaza, per Al Jazeera's live coverage](https://www.aljazeera.com/news/liveblog/2026/7/6/iran-war-live-tehran-set-for-khameneis-procession-israel-bombs-lebanon). The same coverage carried a message from detained Kamal Adwan Hospital director Dr Hussam Abu Safia saying he did not expect to survive detention.

In a potentially significant shift in Gaza's governance and aid landscape, [Hamas announced the dissolution of its Gaza governing body on 6 July](https://www.aljazeera.com/news/2026/7/6/hamas-announces-dissolution-of-gaza-governing-body).

The UN human rights body [demanded the release of Dr Hussam Abu Safia, the Palestinian doctor detained by Israel](https://www.aljazeera.com/news/2026/7/6/un-rights-body-demands-release-of-palestinian-doctor-detained-by-israel), amid escalating concern for medical workers held in detention.

In Iran, [a massive funeral procession was held in Tehran for slain Supreme Leader Ali Khamenei on 6 July](https://www.aljazeera.com/news/2026/7/6/khamenei-family-mourns-but-mojtabas-absence-fuels-public-insecurity) amid the continuing US-Israel war with Iran, with residents reporting deepening insecurity — a conflict with mounting humanitarian consequences across the region.

In North Africa, [Moroccan authorities announced on 6 July they had dismantled an ISIL-linked cell planning attacks](https://www.aljazeera.com/news/2026/7/6/morocco-says-it-has-dismantled-isil-linked-cell-planning-attack), arresting 10 suspects.

## 🌐 Tier 3 — Rest of the World
In Peru, [left-wing candidate Roberto Sanchez conceded the presidential vote to Keiko Fujimori on 6 July](https://www.aljazeera.com/news/2026/7/6/left-wing-candidate-roberto-sanchez-concedes-peru-vote-to-keiko-fujimori), an outcome being watched for its implications for stability and for the millions of displaced Venezuelans hosted in Peru.

As the UN met to discuss genocide prevention, [Al Jazeera published a 6 July analysis of six instances where the UN failed to prevent genocide](https://www.aljazeera.com/news/2026/7/6/un-discusses-prevention-of-genocide-six-times-it-failed-to-do-just-that), underscoring ongoing debates about the effectiveness of international protection mechanisms.
