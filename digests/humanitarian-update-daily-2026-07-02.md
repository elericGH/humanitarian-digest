## 🇦🇺 Australian Priority Alerts

Severe winter weather is affecting southeastern Australia today, with riverine flood warnings active near [Albury, NSW](https://disasteralert.pdc.org/disasteralert/?hazard_id=477059), [Wangaratta](https://disasteralert.pdc.org/disasteralert/?hazard_id=474121) and [south of Shepparton](https://disasteralert.pdc.org/disasteralert/?hazard_id=475805) in Victoria, alongside damaging wind warnings for Newcastle (NSW), Geelong (Vic), Port Augusta (SA) and Burnie (Tas), and severe weather warnings covering Canberra, Melbourne and Mount Gambier, per the [Pacific Disaster Center's Disaster Alert system](https://www.pdc.org/).

The long-running Eastern Australia drought remains an active GDACS event, now tracked for 18 consecutive weeks ([GDACS drought report](http://gdacs.org/report.aspx?eventid=1018457&episodeid=6&eventtype=DR)). Australian agencies' appeals context: no new appeal announcements were published by ACFID, Emergency Action Alliance or DFAT in the last 24 hours; [ACFID's Venezuela Earthquake Appeal](https://acfid.asn.au/venezuela-earthquake-appeal/) and [Ebola outbreak appeal](https://acfid.asn.au/act-now-to-contain-the-ebola-outbreak/) (both last updated 29 June) and [EAA's Venezuela monitoring page](https://emergencyaction.org.au/venezuela-earthquake/) remain the most current Australian-sector responses.

## 🌏 Tier 1 — Pacific & Asia-Pacific

A cluster of moderate earthquakes struck the Pacific in the past 24 hours: a [M5.7 near Tonga (01 Jul 21:13 UTC)](http://gdacs.org/report.aspx?eventid=1549627&episodeid=1715727&eventtype=EQ), a [M5.6 south of Suva, Fiji (01 Jul 10:42 UTC)](http://gdacs.org/report.aspx?eventid=1549555&episodeid=1715635&eventtype=EQ) and a [M6.0 off Noda, Japan (01 Jul 12:08 UTC)](http://gdacs.org/report.aspx?eventid=1549565&episodeid=1715647&eventtype=EQ). All are currently GDACS green alerts with no major damage reported.

In the Philippines, the Pacific Disaster Center has an active WARNING for [Mayon volcano](https://disasteralert.pdc.org/disasteralert/?hazard_id=389474), while the response to the [June 2026 Philippines earthquake and tsunami](https://reliefweb.int/disaster/eq-2026-000083-phl) remains ongoing per ReliefWeb's disaster tracker.

East Asia Forum published analysis on 1 July arguing that [Southeast Asian states are hedging as trust in Washington wanes](https://eastasiaforum.org/2026/07/01/southeast-asia-hedges-as-trust-in-washington-wanes/), with implications for regional crisis-response cooperation, alongside a companion piece on [China's positioning in the Iran war](https://eastasiaforum.org/2026/07/01/china-gains-by-standing-aside-in-the-iran-war/) and the risks to energy flows through the Strait of Hormuz.

## 🌍 Tier 2 — Middle East & Africa

Qatar reported "positive progress" as the US and Iran wrapped up indirect technical talks in Doha, with Iran agreeing to open a communication channel on a memorandum of understanding with Washington ([Al Jazeera live coverage, 2 July](https://www.aljazeera.com/news/liveblog/2026/7/2/iran-war-live-positive-progress-as-us-tehran-wrap-up-indirect-talks)).

In Lebanon, Al Jazeera reports a mounting [psychological toll as villages are erased by Israel's war](https://www.aljazeera.com/news/2026/7/1/psychological-toll-mounts-as-lebanese-villages-erased-by-israels-war), with displaced residents losing the physical and emotional anchors of their identity; its live coverage also reported an [Israeli drone strike in southern Lebanon](https://www.aljazeera.com/news/liveblog/2026/7/2/iran-war-live-positive-progress-as-us-tehran-wrap-up-indirect-talks) in the past day.

On the DR Congo/Uganda Ebola outbreak, Al Jazeera published a 2 July editorial from [the front line of Congo's Ebola outbreak](https://www.aljazeera.com/editorial/2026/7/2/on-the-front-line-of-congos-ebola-outbreak), as the [ReliefWeb-tracked outbreak response](https://reliefweb.int/disaster/ep-2026-000071-cod) continues.

## 🌐 Tier 3 — Rest of the World

Aid workers in Venezuela describe "a war zone" and fear a health crisis in the aftermath of the late-June M7.2/M7.5 earthquakes ([Al Jazeera, 2 July](https://www.aljazeera.com/news/2026/7/2/a-war-zone-venezuela-aid-workers-fear-health-crisis-after-earthquakes)); a companion feature reports [volunteers taking charge of the response](https://www.aljazeera.com/features/longform/2026/7/1/as-venezuela-responds-to-earthquake-devastation-volunteers-take-charge). Copernicus EMS rapid mapping for the M7.5 remains active ([GDACS](https://gdacs.org/)).

Kyiv came under Russian drone attack overnight after President Zelenskyy warned of a "massive Russian strike," with fires reported in residential buildings ([Al Jazeera, 2 July](https://www.aljazeera.com/news/2026/7/2/kyiv-attacked-after-ukraines-zelenskyy-warns-of-massive-russian-strike)). The UN Humanitarian Coordinator for Ukraine also condemned a fresh wave of attacks on energy infrastructure ([ReliefWeb updates](https://reliefweb.int/updates)).

## 🚨 Sudden Onset Hazards

Super Typhoon BAVI-26, with sustained winds of 259 km/h, is active in the western Pacific ([GDACS report, 02 Jul 00:00 UTC](http://gdacs.org/report.aspx?eventid=1001279&episodeid=5&eventtype=TC)). Two weaker systems are also being tracked: [Tropical Storm DOUGLAS-26 (74 km/h, 02 Jul 03:00 UTC)](http://gdacs.org/report.aspx?eventid=1001280&episodeid=4&eventtype=TC) and [TEN-26 (74 km/h)](http://gdacs.org/report.aspx?eventid=1001281&episodeid=2&eventtype=TC).

Earthquakes in the past 24 hours include [M5.7 Tonga](http://gdacs.org/report.aspx?eventid=1549627&episodeid=1715727&eventtype=EQ), [M5.6 Fiji](http://gdacs.org/report.aspx?eventid=1549555&episodeid=1715635&eventtype=EQ), [M6.0 Japan](http://gdacs.org/report.aspx?eventid=1549565&episodeid=1715647&eventtype=EQ) and [M5.5 Afghanistan (01 Jul 17:57 UTC)](http://gdacs.org/report.aspx?eventid=1549600&episodeid=1715684&eventtype=EQ) — all green alerts. GDACS also lists active flood events in the [United States](http://gdacs.org/report.aspx?eventid=1103888&episodeid=35&eventtype=FL), [Ghana](http://gdacs.org/report.aspx?eventid=1103954&episodeid=3&eventtype=FL) and [India](http://gdacs.org/report.aspx?eventid=1103972&episodeid=2&eventtype=FL) (all updated 30 June).
