## 🇦🇺 Australian Alerts

**Australia silent on Gaza aid ban** — Foreign Minister Penny Wong has remained silent on Israel's suspension of 37 international NGOs in Gaza, despite mounting pressure from humanitarian groups. The organisations — including Doctors Without Borders, IRC, and Mercy Corps — were ordered to withdraw by 1 March 2026 after refusing to hand over staff biodata. An Israeli High Court injunction temporarily froze the ban on 27 February, but the situation remains unresolved. [Michael West Media](https://michaelwest.com.au/ngos-warn-of-catastrophic-impact-penny-wong-doesnt-care)

**OCHA: Asia-Pacific bears 75% of global disaster burden** — A new OCHA report released this week confirms Asia-Pacific accounts for three-quarters of all disaster-affected people globally, with 70 million affected per year — a key finding with direct relevance to Australia's aid priorities in the region. [PINA / OCHA](https://pina.com.fj/2026/03/11/asia-pacific-accounts-for-three-quarters-of-global-disaster-affected-people-ocha/)

**No new emergency announcements from ACFID, EAA, or the Foreign Minister in the past 24 hours.**

## What's New Today

- **Myanmar** — OCHA's Humanitarian Update #51 (9 March) reports ongoing conflict and access constraints compounding earthquake recovery; IOM is urgently seeking USD $17.3 million for communities worst-affected by the earthquake that struck last year. Over 16 million people require humanitarian assistance in 2026. [OCHA Myanmar](https://www.unocha.org/publications/report/myanmar/myanmar-humanitarian-update-no51-9-march-2026) | [IOM](https://www.iom.int/news/iom-urgently-seeks-usd-173-million-support-communities-hit-hardest-myanmar-earthquake)

- **Gaza** — Israeli authorities have closed all crossings and suspended humanitarian staff rotations, medical evacuations and the return of internally displaced people. UNRWA's head Philippe Lazzarini described the move as "a new chokehold on Gaza." Flour prices surged 270% in two days as households stockpile food. [UN News](https://news.un.org/en/story/2026/03/1167069)

- **Middle East (Iran/Lebanon/Syria)** — UNHCR's Emergency Flash Update #3 (9 March) reports Lebanon sheltering 58,000 people across 320 collective sites, and Iran displacement exceeding initial estimates of 100,000. UNHCR requires $454.2 million in 2026 but has received only 15% of needed funding. [ReliefWeb / UNHCR](https://reliefweb.int/report/iran-islamic-republic/unhcr-middle-east-situation-emergency-flash-update-3-9-march-2026)

- **Sudan** — Intensified drone strikes on El Obeid (North Kordofan) and continued fighting between the SAF and RSF across Kordofan and Blue Nile States. The UN has confirmed pockets of famine throughout the country, with the situation described as the world's worst humanitarian calamity. [UN News](https://news.un.org/en/story/2026/03/1167069)

- **Pacific (disease alerts)** — ReliefWeb's epidemic alert map for the Pacific (10 March) flags active disease concerns across multiple island nations; full details available via the OCHA Pacific disease surveillance dashboard. [ReliefWeb](https://reliefweb.int/map/world/epidemic-and-emerging-disease-alerts-pacific-10-march-2026)
