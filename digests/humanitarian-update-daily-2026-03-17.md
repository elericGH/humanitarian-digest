## 🇦🇺 Australian Alerts

**Middle East Appeal launched by Australian aid coalition** — Fifteen Australian charities, including Save the Children Australia, Oxfam Australia, CARE Australia, ADRA, and Act for Peace, have launched a coordinated public fundraising appeal for Lebanon, Iran, Gaza, and the West Bank as regional conflict intensifies. The EAA-aligned appeal aims to raise millions of dollars for food, shelter, water, medical aid, and education. ## What's New Today

- **Middle East — Iran/Lebanon** — Now in its third week, the US-Israeli military campaign in Iran has internally displaced an estimated 3.2 million people within Iran and 700,000 in Lebanon. WHO has verified 18 attacks on healthcare facilities in Iran since 28 February (8 health worker deaths), and 25 in Lebanon (16 deaths). Oil-market shocks are driving up global aid delivery costs. [IOM](https://www.iom.int/escalating-conflict-middle-east-growing-humanitarian-crisis) | [WHO](https://www.who.int/news/item/11-03-2026-conflict-deepens-health-crisis-across-middle-east--who-says)

- **Gaza** — All crossings except Kerem Shalom remain closed. UNRWA is blocked from bringing personnel and aid directly into the Strip; medical evacuations are suspended. Fuel shortages threaten basic operations. Heightened movement restrictions also persist across the West Bank. [UNRWA SitRep #212](https://www.unrwa.org/resources/reports/unrwa-situation-report-212-humanitarian-crisis-gaza-strip-and-occupied-west-bank)

- **Myanmar** — OCHA's 9 March update reports 2.6 million people are being prioritised for life-saving assistance in 2026, still heavily shaped by the March 2025 earthquake response. Underfunding and access restrictions continue. [OCHA Myanmar Update #51](https://www.unocha.org/publications/report/myanmar/myanmar-humanitarian-update-no51-9-march-2026)

- **Asia-Pacific (regional)** — OCHA reports Asia-Pacific accounts for 75% of all globally disaster-affected people. Border tensions between Cambodia and Thailand have displaced over 340,000 people. UNICEF seeks US$39.5M for the region in 2026. [PINA/OCHA](https://pina.com.fj/2026/03/11/asia-pacific-accounts-for-three-quarters-of-global-disaster-affected-people-ocha/)

- **Sudan** — Drone attacks have intensified in El Obeid (North Kordofan) and Kurmuk (Blue Nile State); a medical facility in El Obeid was hit, injuring 12. The Iran conflict is disrupting Sudan's fertiliser imports and raising food security concerns. [UN News](https://news.un.org/en/story/2026/03/1167069)

## 🔴 Escalation Watch

The Middle East regional war (now Day 17+) is creating cascading humanitarian consequences beyond the immediate conflict zone: global oil shocks are raising the cost of aid operations in Sudan, Myanmar, and Afghanistan, and have severely disrupted shipping supply chains. The World Food Programme warns this is affecting life-saving programs globally.
