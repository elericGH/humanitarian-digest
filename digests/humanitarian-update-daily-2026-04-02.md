# ⚡ Daily Humanitarian Update — Thursday, 2 April 2026
*24-hour scan | Australian Interest Priority*

## 🇦🇺 Australian Alerts

No new Australian priority releases in the past 24 hours. The most recent announcement was on 21 March 2026, when Australia committed an additional $5 million in humanitarian assistance to Lebanon — $3 million to WFP for emergency food assistance and $2 million to UNICEF for nutrition, water, and health services. This brings Australia's total humanitarian assistance for Gaza and Lebanon to over $135 million since October 2023. ## What's New Today

- **Bangladesh / Rohingya** — WFP has slashed food assistance for approximately 1.2 million Rohingya refugees in Bangladesh's camps, effective 1 April, with some receiving as little as $7 per month; the 2026 program is only 19% funded and dozens of refugees staged protests against the cuts. [Source](https://www.wkms.org/npr-news/2026-04-01/food-assistance-slashed-for-hundreds-of-thousands-of-rohingya-refugees-trapped-in-bangladesh-camps)

- **Myanmar** — Ongoing conflict and the aftermath of the March 2025 earthquake have left 16 million people requiring life-saving assistance and more than 3.6 million internally displaced; the 2026 Humanitarian Needs and Response Plan seeks $890 million with less than a quarter pledged. [Source](https://www.ifrc.org/press-release/gruelling-conditions-myanmar-humanitarian-needs-remain-high-following-devastating)

- **Gaza** — Despite a ceasefire in place since October 2025, 1.6 million people (77% of the population) face high levels of acute food insecurity through April 2026, with only 260 of 619 health service points functioning and ongoing access restrictions for aid organisations. [Source](https://www.ochaopt.org/content/gaza-humanitarian-response-situation-report-no-69)

- **Lebanon** — Conflict escalation since early March 2026 — the worst deterioration since the November 2024 ceasefire — has forced over 700,000 people to flee; attacks on healthcare continue with seven incidents recorded over a single weekend, killing at least nine health workers. [Source](https://news.fundsforngos.org/2026/03/10/lebanon-crisis-deepens-as-700000-people-displaced-irc-warns/)

- **Sudan** — Cholera is spreading rapidly across multiple states, with MSF recording 40 deaths and treating more than 2,300 patients in the past week; El Fasher in North Darfur remains under siege with some families reportedly surviving on animal feed. [Source](https://news.un.org/en/story/2026/03/1167099)

- **Yemen** — Over 22 million people require humanitarian assistance in 2026 — an increase of 2.8 million on the prior year — with WFP activities suspended in Houthi-controlled areas and detention of UN personnel continuing to severely curtail aid delivery. [Source](https://www.securitycouncilreport.org/monthly-forecast/2026-04/yemen-88.php)

## 🔴 Escalation Watch

WFP's food assistance cuts for Rohingya in Bangladesh took effect on 1 April, reducing support to as low as $7/month per person for the world's largest stateless population with only 19% of 2026 funding secured. Authorities warn that refugees may attempt dangerous sea crossings to Malaysia in search of food and work.
