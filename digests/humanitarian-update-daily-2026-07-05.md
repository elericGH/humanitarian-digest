## 🌏 Tier 1 — Pacific & Asia-Pacific

Super Typhoon Bavi has intensified into a Category 5 system with maximum sustained winds of 278 km/h and is bearing down on Guam and the Northern Mariana Islands, with around 50,000 people exposed to Category 1 or higher winds. GDACS issued its latest Red alert episode at 06:00 UTC today, with model guidance indicating rainfall of up to 974 mm over the Marianas ([GDACS](https://gdacs.org/report.aspx?episodeid=18&eventid=1001279&eventtype=TC)).

The Pacific Disaster Center escalated its warnings for the Marianas overnight, issuing coastal flood and high surf warnings for [Guam and the Northern Mariana Islands](https://disasteralert.pdc.org/disasteralert/?hazard_id=479631) within the last hour alongside its ongoing [Super Typhoon Bavi warning](https://disasteralert.pdc.org/disasteralert/?hazard_id=476585). A wildfire advisory was also active near Asan, Guam, complicating pre-landfall preparedness.

Tropical Cyclone Maysak made its final approach to northern Viet Nam in the past 24 hours as a tropical storm with winds of 93 km/h, with GDACS issuing its latest Orange alert episode at 18:00 UTC on 4 July. Storm surge of up to 1.3 m was recorded in official guidance (model runs suggested up to 4.1 m) with forecast rainfall totals as high as 808 mm across Viet Nam, southern China and Laos — a region GDACS rates as high vulnerability ([GDACS](https://gdacs.org/report.aspx?episodeid=13&eventid=1001281&eventtype=TC)).

## 🌍 Tier 2 — Middle East & Africa

Funeral ceremonies for Iran's late supreme leader Ali Khamenei entered a second day on 5 July, drawing millions of mourners in Tehran amid the fragile aftermath of the US-Israel war with Iran; regional leaders including Turkiye's Erdogan said the world had "breathed a sigh of relief" after the Iran-US deal ([Al Jazeera live coverage](https://www.aljazeera.com/news/liveblog/2026/7/5/iran-war-live-huge-crowds-mourn-khamenei-trump-vows-calm-during-funeral)).

In Lebanon, the army reported removing unexploded Israeli bombs in the country's south on 5 July — a reminder of the explosive-ordnance contamination threatening civilians as displaced families return ([Al Jazeera](https://www.aljazeera.com/news/liveblog/2026/7/5/iran-war-live-huge-crowds-mourn-khamenei-trump-vows-calm-during-funeral?update=4735896)).

In Cameroon, a feature published 5 July highlights the national campaign to register every child's birth, a protection issue that determines access to education, healthcare and legal identity for children in a country still managing displacement and conflict in its northern and western regions ([Al Jazeera](https://www.aljazeera.com/news/2026/7/5/a-name-a-document-a-future-cameroons-fight-to-register-every-child)).

## 🌐 Tier 3 — Rest of the World

The Kremlin said on 5 July that US President Trump offered to help end the Russia-Ukraine war during a nearly 90-minute call with President Putin — a development with significant implications for humanitarian access and civilian protection in Ukraine, where attacks on energy infrastructure have continued to kill and injure civilians ([Al Jazeera](https://www.aljazeera.com/news/2026/7/5/trump-offers-to-help-end-ukraine-war-in-long-call-with-putin-kremlin-says)).

Severe weather and an extreme heatwave disrupted the United States' 250th anniversary celebrations on 4-5 July, underscoring the season's compounding extreme-heat risks across the northern hemisphere ([Al Jazeera](https://www.aljazeera.com/news/2026/7/5/severe-weather-disrupts-uss-250th-celebrations)).

**Tropical Cyclone Maysak (MAYSAK-26) — GDACS Orange alert.** Tropical storm affecting Viet Nam, southern China and Laos; storm surge up to 1.3 m and extreme rainfall. Latest episode 04 Jul 18:00 UTC ([GDACS](https://gdacs.org/report.aspx?episodeid=13&eventid=1001281&eventtype=TC)).
