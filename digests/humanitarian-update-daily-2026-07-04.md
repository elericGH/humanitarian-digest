## 🌏 Tier 1 — Pacific & Asia-Pacific

**Super Typhoon Bavi threatens Guam and the Northern Mariana Islands.** GDACS has issued an overall Red alert for Tropical Cyclone BAVI-26, a Category 5 system with maximum sustained winds of 278 km/h and roughly 50,000 people exposed in Category 1-or-higher winds across Guam and the Northern Marianas; model guidance points to rainfall totals of 440–870 mm ([GDACS](https://gdacs.org/report.aspx?eventid=1001279&episodeid=12&eventtype=TC)).

The Pacific Disaster Center's DisasterAWARE platform is carrying active watches for [Super Typhoon Bavi](https://disasteralert.pdc.org/disasteralert/?hazard_id=476585) as well as [flood](https://disasteralert.pdc.org/disasteralert/?hazard_id=478177) and [coastal flood](https://disasteralert.pdc.org/disasteralert/?hazard_id=478272) watches for Guam and the Northern Mariana Islands, all refreshed within the past 24 hours ([PDC](https://www.pdc.org)).

**Significant earthquakes across the region.** In the past 24 hours GDACS registered a M6.2 earthquake in Indonesia ([GDACS](http://gdacs.org/report.aspx?eventid=1549862&episodeid=1715971&eventtype=EQ)) and a M6.1 earthquake in Japan ([GDACS](http://gdacs.org/report.aspx?eventid=1549877&episodeid=1715989&eventtype=EQ)), alongside a M5.9 event in Russia's far east ([GDACS](http://gdacs.org/report.aspx?eventid=1549901&episodeid=1716015&eventtype=EQ)). No major humanitarian impact had been flagged for these events at time of writing.

## 🌍 Tier 2 — Middle East & Africa

**Iran.** Day one of the funeral of Supreme Leader Ali Khamenei concluded in Tehran as dignitaries continued to arrive, with the country still reeling from the US-Israel war; the UK and France issued a joint statement calling the Strait of Hormuz a "vital artery for the global economy" ([Al Jazeera live coverage](https://www.aljazeera.com/news/liveblog/2026/7/3/iran-war-live-tehran-slams-us-ahead-of-huge-funeral-for-ali-khamenei)).

**Lebanon.** Human rights groups said the recently signed Lebanon-Israel framework agreement betrays "victims of war crimes" by sidelining accountability for violations committed during hostilities ([Al Jazeera](https://www.aljazeera.com/news/2026/7/3/lebanon-israel-deal-betrays-victims-of-war-crimes-rights-groups-say)).

**Yemen.** The Houthis threatened Saudi Arabia after an alleged airspace intrusion, raising fears of renewed regional escalation with direct consequences for aid access in Yemen ([Al Jazeera](https://www.aljazeera.com/news/2026/7/3/yemens-houthis-threaten-saudi-arabia-after-alleged-airspace-intrusion)).

**Occupied Palestinian Territory.** A Palestinian youth was shot during confrontations with Israeli forces in Bethlehem and settlers attacked activists and Palestinians trying to access their land in the West Bank ([Al Jazeera live coverage](https://www.aljazeera.com/news/liveblog/2026/7/3/iran-war-live-tehran-slams-us-ahead-of-huge-funeral-for-ali-khamenei)). ReliefWeb's latest headlines also feature NRC/Shelter Cluster findings that shelter restrictions leave nearly one million people in Gaza — about 170,000 households — enduring summer heat in tents ([ReliefWeb](https://reliefweb.int/report/occupied-palestinian-territory/gaza-israels-shelter-restrictions-force-nearly-million-people-endure-summer-heat-tents-nrc)).

## 🌐 Tier 3 — Rest of the World

**Ukraine.** The ICRC said intensifying hostilities have claimed dozens of civilian lives and are deepening trauma for civilians, in a statement issued 3 July ([ICRC](https://www.icrc.org/en/statement/ukraine-intensifying-hostilities-claim-dozens-lives-deepen-trauma-civilians)). Al Jazeera reported a fresh wave of Russian missile and drone attacks on energy infrastructure, including strikes on Kyiv, amid claims that Russia's ground advance has stalled at heavy cost ([Al Jazeera](https://www.aljazeera.com/news/2026/7/3/russian-advance-collapses-in-ukraine-as-anxiety-rises-in-moscow)).

**Venezuela.** Reporting from the ground describes survivors of the late-June earthquakes still struggling with the aftermath — collapsed housing, ongoing search efforts and displacement — as the humanitarian response continues ([Al Jazeera](https://www.aljazeera.com/news/longform/2026/7/3/a-nightmare-strugging-with-the-aftermath-of-venezuelas-earthquakes)).

**United States.** Thousands of people were forced to evacuate in Colorado as a wildfire spread ([Al Jazeera](https://www.aljazeera.com/news/2026/7/3/thousands-forced-to-evacuate-in-colorado-as-wildfire-spreads)).

## 🚨 Sudden Onset Hazards

**Super Typhoon Bavi (GDACS Red alert).** Category 5, max winds 278 km/h, ~50,000 people exposed in Guam and the Northern Mariana Islands; associated flood and coastal-flood watches are active ([GDACS](https://gdacs.org/report.aspx?eventid=1001279&episodeid=12&eventtype=TC); [PDC](https://disasteralert.pdc.org/disasteralert/?hazard_id=476585)).

**Earthquakes (3 July UTC).** M6.2 Indonesia ([GDACS](http://gdacs.org/report.aspx?eventid=1549862&episodeid=1715971&eventtype=EQ)), M6.1 Japan ([GDACS](http://gdacs.org/report.aspx?eventid=1549877&episodeid=1715989&eventtype=EQ)), M5.9 Russia ([GDACS](http://gdacs.org/report.aspx?eventid=1549901&episodeid=1716015&eventtype=EQ)).

**Wildfires.** A forest fire in Portugal has burned about 16,896 ha ([GDACS](http://gdacs.org/report.aspx?eventid=1029260&episodeid=4&eventtype=WF)); a fast-moving wildfire in Colorado forced thousands to evacuate ([Al Jazeera](https://www.aljazeera.com/news/2026/7/3/thousands-forced-to-evacuate-in-colorado-as-wildfire-spreads)). Weaker tropical systems Maysak and Douglas also remain under GDACS monitoring ([GDACS](https://gdacs.org)).
